Project Manager

ARE YOU LOOKING TO CREATE AN IMPACT ?


Project Managers have the primary responsibility to develop plans, implement strategies, and complete the project. They also need to ensure that the project is completed on time and within budget.


What do we want?

Project managers are often responsible for managing several moving parts of a project simultaneously. They work with a range of internal and external stakeholders.


Planning budget, hiring and managing staff, conducting with meeting heads, implementing policies, monitoriing compliance and state regulations to name a few.

About the work


  • Creating a construction schedule
  • Assigning tasks to contractors and subcontractors
  • Tracking project activities
  • Managing relationships with key stakeholders
  • Developing the construction project with architects, engineers, and trade workers
  • Ordering and managing materials and equipment
  • Negotiating vendor contracts

Qualifications


  • Candidates who have experience preferably in construction
  • Multi-tasking skills
  • Tracking project activities
  • Effective oral, written, and reading communication skills

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS


  • 2 years of experience
  • Appropriate license
