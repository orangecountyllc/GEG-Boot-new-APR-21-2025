Construction Project Manager

ARE YOU LOOKING TO CREATE AN IMPACT ?


You are empowered and entrusted to deliver the quality results that best suits the unique needs of your customers.


What do we expect?

The Construction Manager manages all functions of the construction job site as it relates to building quality residential homes.

Supervise construction from dirt through sale while maximizing profits and maintaining excellence.

The Manager insures quality assurance and customer satisfaction are maintained throughout the construction process.


Applications Are Now Being Accepted (Site Manager)

Opportunities with Equity Group

About the work

  • Plan and direct staff and partners in the construction and completion of quality residential homes with strong commitment to executing the business model to 100% Complete
  • Responsible for milestone report (slab to frame, frame to mechanical, drywall to cabinet and cabinet to final)
  • Schedule and monitor trade partners during the entire construction process from start to closing. Informs and trains trade partners regarding company policies and procedures, including safety and OSHA compliance
  • Monitor subdivision cleanliness and hold trade partners responsible for daily clean-up
  • Interact with Architecture, Engineering, Client, Purchasing and consider their input/deadlines during the process
  • Monitor and resolve customer or construction concerns to facilitate smooth final closing process
  • Deliver a consistent and effective homeowner orientation to ensure the buyer experiences a positive transition to their new home
  • Facilitate weekly safety meetings with other Superintendents, staff and trade partners
  • Ensure Quality Checkpoints are completed and provides daily status updates on the construction progress on each unit
  • Drives the permit and inspection sign-off process on a timely basis
  • Responsible for inspection process in assigned homes and interacts with VA, FHA, City, County, State and Conventional inspectors involved in the homebuilding process break

Qualifications

  • 2 - 5 years of construction experience with a production homebuilder
  • Strong experience in delivering high quality customer service programs
  • Strong people skills, including the capability to handle various personalities and develop professional relationships
  • Action oriented, with the drive to push projects and tasks to successful closure
  • Proven ability of being customer centric by seeking solutions from the customer’s perspective

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS

  • 2 years of experience
  • Appropriate license
